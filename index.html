About

IEEE Education Week Sri Lanka is a unique opportunity for students and undergraduates to connect with top professionals and educators from the world of engineering and technology. It will be held at TRACE Expert City on the 07th of April this year, and will provide educational programs and resources for students, educators, and technical professionals. This event is the perfect chance to expand your knowledge, enhance your skills, and build your network. IEEE Education Week Sri Lanka will feature a seminar on higher education pathways for students, an open forum on education, technical sessions, panel discussions, industry stalls, IEEE awareness sessions, etc. This event is also a showcase of all of the amazing work that IEEE volunteers and members do every day to promote education at every level.

Why You Should Join The Event

Booths

Exhibitors often represent educational institutions, organizations, and companies that are leaders in the field of education. Visiting these booths can provide attendees with a chance to learn about new products and services.

Experience

Provides an opportunity for individuals to learn about the latest trends, research, and innovations in the field of education, connect with like-minded individuals, and improve their skills and knowledge.

Networking

Allows attendees to participate in presentations, workshops, and panel discussions where they can meet new people, share ideas, and discuss issues related to education that can lead to future opportunities.

Resources

Offers a range of resources and opportunities to help individuals develop their skills and knowledge.

Cutting-edge Technologies

Presents a unique opportunity to learn about current and emerging technologies through a plethora of technical sessions, group activities, hands-on workshops, panel discussions, etc.

Proceed Forward

The economic crisis of the country has had a major impact on engineers and technologists, and now is the time to find the training and educational opportunities they need.
