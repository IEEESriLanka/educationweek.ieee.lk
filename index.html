IEEE Education Week Sri Lanka - 2024

IEEE Education Week Sri Lanka 2024 is a week-long event that aims to provide a platform for students, educators, and professionals to engage in discussions on the latest trends and technologies in education.

  • April 24th & 25th
  • 10+ Speakers
  • Trace Expert City, Colombo
  • Free Entrance
Learn More

About

IEEE Education Week Sri Lanka is a unique opportunity to connect with top professionals and educators from the world of Engineering. It will be held at TRACE Expert City on the 24th & 25th of April this year and will provide educational programs and resources for students, educators, and technical professionals. This event is the perfect chance to expand your knowledge, enhance your skills, and build your network. There will be a STEM exhibition, teachers' training session, seminar on higher education pathways for students, technical sessions, panel discussions, etc. It is a great way to showcase all of the amazing work that IEEE volunteers and members do every day to promote education at every level.
