About

IEEE Education Week Sri Lanka is a unique opportunity for students and undergraduates to connect with top professionals and educators from the world of engineering and technology. It will be held at TRACE Expert City on the 07th of April this year, and will provide educational programs and resources for students, educators, and technical professionals. This event is the perfect chance to expand your knowledge, enhance your skills, and build your network. IEEE Education Week Sri Lanka will feature a seminar on higher education pathways for students, an open forum on education, technical sessions, panel discussions, industry stalls, IEEE awareness sessions, etc. This event is also a showcase of all of the amazing work that IEEE volunteers and members do every day to promote education at every level.

Why You Should Join The Event

🎯 Explore Booths

Meet top educational institutions, leading organizations, and innovators in the education sector. Discover their latest offerings, products, and services that could shape your academic or professional journey.

💡 Gain Valuable Experience

Engage with the latest educational trends, research breakthroughs, and innovative practices. Connect with peers who share your interests and sharpen your knowledge and skills.

🤝 Expand Your Network

Take part in interactive sessions, workshops, and expert panels. Share perspectives, build meaningful connections, and open doors to future academic and career opportunities.

📚 Access Rich Resources

Tap into a wide variety of learning materials, tools, and support systems designed to help you grow and succeed in your field.

🤖 Dive Into Emerging Tech

Get hands-on exposure to cutting-edge technologies through live demos, group challenges, immersive workshops, and tech talks—ideal for staying ahead in a fast-evolving world.

🚀 Take the Next Step

With the current economic challenges affecting the tech and engineering sectors, this is your chance to find crucial training paths and learning opportunities that can help you move forward with confidence.
